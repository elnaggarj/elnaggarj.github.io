My name is Jacob Elnaggar. I completed a Bachelors of Science in Bioinformatics at Baylor University in 2018. My background is in computer science as well as biological science, and I am specifically interested in big data analysis, machine learning, and personalized medicine.

I am currently an MD/PhD Candidate at LSU Health Science Center in New Orleans, LA. I am completing my PhD in the Taylor Lab within The Department of Microbiology, Immunology, & Parasitology. My research is focused on the vaginal microbiome, understanding the changes that take place prior to bacterial vaginosis.
